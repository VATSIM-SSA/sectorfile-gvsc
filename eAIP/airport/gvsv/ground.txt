==== Taxi Out Procedures ====

  * When runway 06 is active, departing traffic shall be instructed to taxi to the runway via C and hold short runway 06. Aircraft may require or request to enter the runway via A and backtrack.

  * When runway 24 is active, departing traffic shall be instructed to taxi to the runway via A and hold short runway 24.

==== Taxi In Procedures ====

  * If landing runway 06, turning to be executed at the end of the runway, at the turnpad, and backtracking to vacate right on A.

  * If landing runway 24, pilots should vacate at the end via C. Backtrack may be requested to vacate on A.
