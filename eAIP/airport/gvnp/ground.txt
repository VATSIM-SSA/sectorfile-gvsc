==== Taxi Out Procedures ====

  * When runway 03 is active, departing traffic shall be instructed to taxi to the runway via C and A and hold short runway 03.
  * When runway 21 is active, departing traffic shall be instructed to taxi to the runway via C and B and hold short runway 21.

==== Taxi In Procedures ====

  * If landing runway 03, turning to be executed at the end of the runway, at the turnpad, and backtracking to vacate right on B.
  * If landing runway 21, turning to be executed at the end of the runway, at the turnpad, and backtracking to vacate left on A.
